Chocolate Chip Pancakes

These fluffy chocolate chip pancakes are perfect for a sweet breakfast or brunch treat. Easy to make and loved by all ages!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar.
  2. Gradually add milk and stir until smooth.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Cook on a hot greased pan until golden on both sides.
